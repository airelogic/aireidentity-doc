    Apply policy to tenant role

    To map a tenant policy to a tenant role

    Once you have created a new policy and the accompanying statements you will then be able to apply them to the roles you would like them to take effect upon. To do this select ‘Roles’ from the navigation screen, then select the edit icon (Pencil) to edit the role you wish to add the policy to.

    Once you have selected the ‘Edit’ icon you will then see the selected Role in edit mode, from here you will see a drop-down field under Policies. Click on the drop-down menu, and you will be presented with the available policies that can be applied against the role. Once the correct policy or policies have been selected click the ‘ Update’ button.
